# Portfolio Items & Packages – project-catalog

## Driver Scoring Automation (Typeform → Zapier → Notion)

- **Story:** Typeform intake → Zapier steps enrich with DMV/insurance data, call OpenAI for narrative risk explanation, and create structured Notion records with tasks and audit notes. Slack alerts only when thresholds are crossed.
- **Key Outcomes:**
  - Risk scores + follow-up tasks in Notion within 2 minutes.
  - Contextual Slack alerts when risk thresholds trigger.
  - Audit-ready history of every change and document link.
- **Stack:** Typeform, Zapier, OpenAI, Notion, Slack.

## N8N Messaging Agent Platform

- **Story:** n8n orchestrates SMS/email responses the minute a lead appears in Airtable/CRM; AI drafts personalized copy; Slack escalations for VIPs; all synced back to Airtable.
- **Key Outcomes:**
  - Sub-2-minute response times.
  - Lift in qualified appointments from AI-personalized scripts.
  - Airtable holds full conversation logs.
- **Stack:** n8n, Airtable, Twilio, SendGrid, OpenAI, Slack.

## Scraping & Lead Gen Pipelines

- **Story:** Playwright-based scrapers with firmographic enrichment and email verification, delivering daily lead batches to HubSpot/Slack. GitHub Actions scheduling keeps it running.
- **Key Outcomes:**
  - 500+ verified leads/day.
  - Slack/HubSpot delivery every morning.
  - Monitoring catches markup changes.
- **Stack:** Python, Playwright, Pandas, Clearbit/Apollo, Slack, HubSpot/CSV.

## AI Integration & Security Audit Bundle

- **Story:** Pair one production automation/chatbot build with a prompt-security audit (guardrails inspired by prompt-defenders). Red-team prompts, review secrets, RBAC, monitoring hooks.
- **Key Outcomes:**
  - Revenue-driving AI workflow delivered with hardened prompts.
  - Credential/webhook/RBAC review documented for compliance.
  - Monitoring alerts on prompt drift or config changes.
- **Stack:** Zapier/n8n, OpenAI/LangChain, Prompt-defenders, Slack/GitHub alerts.
