# Upwork Service Descriptions

## Driver Scoring Automation

**Rapid Intake Setup** → **Risk Ops Workspace** → **Enterprise Driver Compliance Hub**

Typeform intake flows that automatically enrich applicant data with DMV/insurance records, generate AI-powered risk narratives, and populate structured Notion or GoHighLevel workspaces with follow-up tasks. Slack alerts trigger only when risk thresholds cross critical lines. Works with GoHighLevel, n8n, Zapier, or Make. Includes Loom handover and short internal training for your team. Perfect for insurance, rideshare, and logistics teams needing audit-ready scoring within 2 minutes.

## N8N Messaging Agent Platform (GoHighLevel + n8n)

**Single-Channel Bot** → **Dual-Channel Pod** → **Concierge Messaging Ops**

n8n workflows that detect new leads in GoHighLevel (or CRM/Airtable) and instantly reply via SMS or email with AI-personalized copy. Includes missed-call recovery automation, pipeline updates, and lead routing. VIP escalations go to Slack; full conversation logs sync back to your database. Includes Loom handover and short internal training for your team. Real estate, SaaS, and recruitment teams see lift in qualified appointments and sub-2-minute response times.

## Scraping & Lead Gen Pipelines

**Single Marketplace Scraper** → **Verified Lead Engine** → **Always-On Lead Factory**

Production-grade Python/Playwright scrapers with firmographic enrichment and email verification. GitHub Actions runs daily; fresh leads land in HubSpot/Slack every morning. Monitors for markup changes so you stay ahead of site updates. Ideal for B2B prospecting, vendor discovery, and market research.

Pair any production automation or chatbot build with a prompt-security audit. We red-team your prompts, review API keys/webhooks/RBAC, and set up monitoring alerts for prompt drift or config changes. Ensures your revenue-driving AI workflows stay compliant and resilient against prompt injection.
