# Upwork Service Descriptions

## Driver Scoring Automation

**Rapid Intake Setup** → **Risk Ops Workspace** → **Enterprise Driver Compliance Hub**

Typeform intake flows that automatically enrich applicant data with DMV/insurance records, generate AI-powered risk narratives, and populate structured Notion workspaces with follow-up tasks. Slack alerts trigger only when risk thresholds cross critical lines. Perfect for insurance, rideshare, and logistics teams needing audit-ready scoring within 2 minutes.

## N8N Messaging Agent Platform

**Single-Channel Bot** → **Dual-Channel Pod** → **Concierge Messaging Ops**

n8n workflows that detect new leads in your CRM/Airtable and instantly reply via SMS or email with AI-personalized copy. VIP escalations go to Slack; full conversation logs sync back to your database. Real estate, SaaS, and recruitment teams see lift in qualified appointments and sub-2-minute response times.

## Scraping & Lead Gen Pipelines

**Single Marketplace Scraper** → **Verified Lead Engine** → **Always-On Lead Factory**

Production-grade Python/Playwright scrapers with firmographic enrichment and email verification. GitHub Actions runs daily; fresh leads land in HubSpot/Slack every morning. Monitors for markup changes so you stay ahead of site updates. Ideal for B2B prospecting, vendor discovery, and market research.

## AI Integration & Security Audit Bundle

**Workflow + Light Audit** → **AI Ops Guardrail Suite** → **Enterprise AI Trust Program**

Pair any production automation or chatbot build with a prompt-security audit. We red-team your prompts, review API keys/webhooks/RBAC, and set up monitoring alerts for prompt drift or config changes. Ensures your revenue-driving AI workflows stay compliant and resilient against prompt injection.
